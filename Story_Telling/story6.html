The Wise Mother

Theme: Good Parenting

Kural: The wealth of a mother is her son’s good conduct. (Kural 70)

In a quiet hamlet, a mother named Meenakshi raised her son, Arun, with great care. From a young age, she taught him honesty, respect for elders, and compassion for all. Whenever he did wrong, she corrected him with patience, not punishment.

When Arun grew older, his classmates began stealing fruits from a neighbor’s farm. They asked him to join, but he refused. “My mother taught me better,” he said firmly. Though mocked, Arun stood by his values.

Years later, Arun became a respected judge known for his fairness and honesty. At every award ceremony, he thanked his mother, saying, “She shaped who I am.”

Meenakshi never had riches, but she raised a son who became the pride of the town. Her true wealth was her son’s character—a lesson every parent remembered.

Moral The best gift parents can give is good character.
